Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This app is designed to be transparent, minimal, and secure.

Camera Access

Our website requests access to your device camera only when you choose to activate it. Camera access is handled directly by your browser and requires your explicit permission.

We never access your camera without your consent.

Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any server.

All photos remain on your device and are processed locally within your browser session.

Third-Party Services

We do not share user data with third parties. No personal information is sold, stored, or distributed.

Security

Camera permissions are managed entirely by your browser. You may revoke access at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if new features are added. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
